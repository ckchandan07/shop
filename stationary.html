Stationary

A stationery shop is a retail store that specializes in selling a wide variety of essential products for writing, organizing, crafting, and office work. These shops cater to students, professionals, artists, and businesses by providing everything from basic writing tools to specialized office supplies.

Common items available in a stationery shop include pens, pencils, markers, erasers, sharpeners, and rulers. For organizational purposes, they offer notebooks, diaries, planners, and files, along with folders, binders, and sticky notes. Paper products such as A4 sheets, envelopes, drawing paper, and chart paper are also a staple in these shops. Many stationery stores stock office supplies like staplers, hole punches, tape dispensers, scissors, and adhesive products.
